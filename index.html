How I work

A simple, repeatable delivery loop.

From first contact to shipped feature, each engagement follows the same cadence — no mystery, no surprises, no mid-project scope drift.

01

Understand

Read your codebase, Frappe version, and data model. Understand what's actually broken before writing a single line.

02

Scope

Define what's in and what's out. Complexity flagged early so there are no "just one more thing" surprises later.

03

Build

Ship in small, testable chunks. Each piece runs in your environment before moving on — not a big-bang delivery.

04

Hand off

Clean code, documented to Frappe conventions. You can maintain it after I leave. No black boxes, no lock-in.

FAQ

Questions people usually ask.

Common concerns surfaced before we start working together. If yours isn't here, just email me.

Do you only work with Frappe / ERPNext?

Frappe is where I'm deepest, but I'm comfortable with any Python backend work, Linux server administration, Docker infrastructure, and database design. If your project is adjacent, reach out.

What does a typical engagement look like?

A short scoping call, then a fixed-scope proposal. I prefer project-based over hourly — clearer expectations on both sides and no clock-watching.

Can you work with our existing ERPNext setup?

Yes. I'll audit your current bench setup, custom apps, and data model before touching anything. I don't refactor for sport — only when it solves a real problem.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Retainers are available for ongoing Frappe maintenance, upgrades, and monitoring. Availability is limited, so reach out early if that's what you need.

What's your current availability?

I take on a small number of projects at a time to keep quality high. Email me and I'll give you a straight answer on timelines — no vague "might be available."

Can you help with Frappe hosting and DevOps?

Yes — Docker setup, server configuration, WireGuard VPN, Traefik, Nginx, swap tuning, and Linux administration are all things I run in production today.
